Services

Pick a full pipeline or specific components. We slot into your way of working.

TMS Setup & Integration

Design, configure, and secure your translation engine.

  • memoQ & Phrase configuration, workflows, roles, SSO.
  • Parser rules to lock placeholders, variables, and non‑translatables.
  • TM/TBX/Glossary setup, maintenance & versioning.
  • API/webhook integrations with CMS, repositories, and ticketing.
  • Automation: pre‑/post‑processing, QA checks, and MT routing.

File Pre‑processing

Clean, normalize, and convert assets before import.

  • Fix broken encoding, hidden text, corrupt styles, rogue fonts.
  • IDML/INDD, PDF to editable, Storyline packages (.story), Rise exports.
  • Structure markup for maximum leverage and consistent segmentation.
  • Automated checks: placeholder integrity, regex QA, non‑translatables.

Desktop Publishing (DTP)

Publish‑ready files across design suites and office tools.

  • Adobe Creative Cloud (InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop, XD).
  • Microsoft Office, Google Workspace, Figma, Canva.
  • Style mirroring, typography for Asian/RTL scripts, layout QA.
  • Hi‑DPI assets, print and digital output, preflight & packaging.

e‑Learning Localization

Localized, accessible courses that look and sound right.

  • Articulate Storyline & Rise 360 builds with mirrored design.
  • Captioning/subtitles (SRT/VTT) and voice‑over synchronization.
